About cloudSrv

Engineers running an
engineering-led firm.

We started cloudSrv because most consulting firms either recruit without engineering depth, or build software without business context. We do both — and we do them together.

Our story

Founded by builders. Run by builders.

cloudSrv was founded in 2023 by senior engineers who had spent over a decade shipping production systems at high-growth product companies. We watched US engineering teams struggle with two parallel problems: hiring senior talent that actually performs, and standing up AI systems that survive past the prototype stage.

We built cloudSrv to solve both — under one roof, with the same engineering bar.

Where we are

Headquartered in Apex, NC.

Our team operates from Apex, North Carolina, with engineering partners and recruiting networks across the United States and globally. We're an E-Verify employer, fully equipped for H-1B, PERM, and L-1 onboarding.

What we believe

Four principles, no exceptions

Senior bar, always

Every engineer we place or deploy has shipped real software in production. No bootcamp shuffling, no fresh juniors masquerading as architects.

Speed without shortcuts

Most engagements start within five business days. Curated shortlists, not blasted resumes. Tight proposals, not 80-slide decks.

Outcomes, not hours

We measure ourselves on what shipped, who got hired, and whether they stayed. Time sheets don't reflect value — outcomes do.

Clean hand-offs

We exit with documentation, training, and an internal owner ready to run the system. The goal is your team's independence, not our dependency.
