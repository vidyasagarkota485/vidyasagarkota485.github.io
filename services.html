What we deliver

Services built for outcomes, not hours.

Two practices, deeply connected: senior IT talent that ships, and AI systems engineered for production.

Practice 01

IT Talent & Recruiting

We don't run a generic staffing desk. cloudSrv recruiters are former engineers themselves, which is why our candidates pass technical bars at top US engineering teams. Every shortlist is curated — never blasted.

Engagement models: permanent placement, contract-to-hire, embedded squads, and PERM/H-1B-aware onboarding for global hires.

Roles we fill: Senior & Staff Engineers, Engineering Managers, Solutions Architects, ML/AI Engineers, Platform/DevOps, Data Engineers, Security Engineers.

What clients get

  • Curated shortlist within 5 business days
  • Technical screen by senior engineers
  • Reference checks & offer support
  • Onboarding playbook for first 90 days
  • Immigration & visa coordination
  • Replacement guarantee on permanent hires
Practice 02

AI Systems Design

Most AI demos die between prototype and production. We've built the difference enough times to make it predictable: real evals, sober latency and cost budgets, observability, and a hand-off your team can actually run.

Where we operate: AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, GCP Vertex, plus open-source stacks (vLLM, Ollama) when self-hosting makes sense.

Outcome focus: shipped systems with measurable accuracy, latency, and unit-economic guarantees — not slide decks.

What we build

  • RAG pipelines with real evals & guardrails
  • Multi-agent platforms & tool-use orchestration
  • Vector infrastructure & hybrid search
  • Model fine-tuning & inference cost tuning
  • LLM observability, tracing, and safety reviews
  • Prompt & eval frameworks for product teams
