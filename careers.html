  1. Senior Solutions Architect


    • ▪ Position: cloudSrv Inc. seeks a Senior Solutions Architect at our Apex, NC headquarters to architect, design, build, and deliver scalable cloud-native and distributed data platforms supporting cloudSrv's enterprise clients.
    • ▪ Duties: Design and build highly scalable real-time data pipelines using Go (GoLang), Apache Kafka, Apache Flink, and MongoDB; architect Delta Lake and Change Data Capture (CDC) solutions leveraging Debezium and Apache Iceberg on AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform; engineer high-throughput API gateways and gRPC services in Go supporting over one million concurrent connections with low latency; migrate legacy monolithic Java/J2EE applications to containerized microservices orchestrated on Kubernetes with ArgoCD CI/CD pipelines; integrate AWS services including S3, Lambda, and DynamoDB; develop Spring Boot microservices and implement Redis caching layers to optimize performance; apply Test Driven Development (TDD), conduct peer code reviews, and manage Helm chart-based release processes; mentor engineering teams and provide technical guidance on architecture, performance tuning, and engineering best practices.
    • ▪ Requirements: Master's degree (or foreign equivalent) in Information Technology, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication Engineering, or a closely related field, plus five (5) years of progressive post-baccalaureate experience in software engineering or solutions architecture. In lieu of a Master's degree, employer will accept a Bachelor's degree (or foreign equivalent) plus seven (7) years of progressive experience.
    • ▪ Required Skills: Go (Goroutines and channels), Apache Kafka, Apache Flink, Spring Boot, Kubernetes, Docker, AWS (S3, Lambda, DynamoDB), MongoDB, PostgreSQL, gRPC, Redis, Jenkins, and ArgoCD CI/CD pipelines.
    • ▪ Alt Occu: Solutions Architect, Software Architect, Engineering Manager, Principal Software Engineer.
    • ▪ Job Site: Apex, NC. May require travel and/or relocation to unanticipated client sites throughout the United States. 40 hrs/week. EOE.
    • ▪ To Apply: Mail resume referencing Job Code SSA-2026-001 to cloudSrv Inc, Attn: HR Department, Toll Brothers at Weddington, 186 Annabelle Branch Ln, Apex, NC 27523 — or email hr@cloudSrv.com.
cloudSrv Inc offers referral bonus or fee upon successful placement of the candidate.
