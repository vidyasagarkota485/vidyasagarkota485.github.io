Senior Solutions Architect

Apex, NC · Full-time, Permanent · 40 hrs / week · Job Code SSA-2026-001
Now Hiring

Position

cloudSrv Inc. seeks a Senior Solutions Architect at our Apex, NC headquarters to architect, design, build, and deliver scalable cloud-native and distributed data platforms supporting cloudSrv's enterprise clients.

Duties

  • Design and build highly scalable real-time data pipelines using Go (GoLang), Apache Kafka, Apache Flink, and MongoDB.
  • Architect Delta Lake and Change Data Capture (CDC) solutions leveraging Debezium and Apache Iceberg on AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.
  • Engineer high-throughput API gateways and gRPC services in Go supporting over one million concurrent connections with low latency.
  • Migrate legacy monolithic Java/J2EE applications to containerized microservices orchestrated on Kubernetes with ArgoCD CI/CD pipelines.
  • Integrate AWS services including S3, Lambda, and DynamoDB.
  • Develop Spring Boot microservices and implement Redis caching layers to optimize performance.
  • Apply Test Driven Development (TDD), conduct peer code reviews, and manage Helm chart-based release processes.
  • Mentor engineering teams and provide technical guidance on architecture, performance tuning, and engineering best practices.

Requirements

Master's degree (or foreign equivalent) in Information Technology, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication Engineering, or a closely related field, plus five (5) years of progressive post-baccalaureate experience in software engineering or solutions architecture. In lieu of a Master's degree, employer will accept a Bachelor's degree (or foreign equivalent) plus seven (7) years of progressive experience.

Required Skills

Apex, NC. May require travel and/or relocation to unanticipated client sites throughout the United States. 40 hrs/week. EOE.
